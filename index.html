ambulance services

If the patient requires medical support during transport you can avail of the 1066 ambulance (chargeable basis) which is fully equipped with life support devices. In case you require the above service, inform the ward staff

emergency rooms

The Emergency Room is one of the most critical areas in the hospital. The protocol at our 24X7 Emergency Medicine Department is designed to respond quicker and have proven outcomes that are on par with the very best.

JehangirHospital

Jehangir Hospital in Pune has been providing quality healthcare services for over 75 years. NABH, NABL and ISO accredited, the 350-bed Jehangir Hospital is renowned for its medical excellence, the dedication of its consultants and the care and compassion of its staff.
