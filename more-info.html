Jehangir Hospital was established in 1946. It began as Jehangir Nursing Home when its founders, Sir Cowasji Jehangir and Lady Hirabai Jehangir, invited the eminent Dr. Edul jee H. Coyaji to set up a facility to offer personalized care of high quality at an affordable price to the people of Poona. On the 6th of February, 1946, it became the city's first significant private hospital with only a few beds.

In 1998, Jehangir Hospital was equipped with 350 beds, tied up with the Apollo Hospitals Group to help manage and modernize the hospital. The Jehangir Hospital - Apollo Hospitals Group synergy provided the best outcome for the patients: superior technology and medical skill combined with quality patient care

Today being one of the oldest and most renowned names in Pune, Jehangir Hospital, with a legacy of providing quality healthcare services for over 75 years, holds a steadfast position of trust and respect in the people's hearts and minds. NABH, NABL, and Nursing Excellence accredited, the 350-bed with 85 fully-equipped ICU beds, Jehangir Hospital is synonymous with medical excellence, quality of its patient care, and affordability.

With 9 Operation Theatres and 24x7 Emergency Department and ambulance service, Jehangir Hospital endures care at every step. Jehangir Hospital is a pioneer of modern-day emergency care in Pune. The hospital guarantees the highest level of skill, expertise, infrastructure and operates one of the best Accident and Emergency Departments in Maharashtra. It provides services for all kinds of emergencies, like Trauma, Non-trauma, Medical or Surgical emergencies, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, and 365 days a year.

Jehangir Hospital is also a top-rated learning centre in Maharashtra for providing a DNB programme for PG students. It is one of the only two colleges in Pune which provide DNB - Emergency Medicine. Other than that, we also offer the DNB programme in Pathology and DNB Orthopaedic Surgery.

Jehangir Hospital is committed to achieving and maintaining excellence in health care services, medical education, and research. The belief that clinical services supported by medical education and research of the highest quality can help both the medical community and our patients is an integral part of our Jehangir ethos. From ISO certified JCDC, which meets the gold standard for research centres worldwide, and HCJMRI, working in the area of infant and children's health, to carry out research, educational, and training programs in the quest of quality health care, we have raised the bar with scientific papers and community programs being done by these teams.

Good health isn't simply the absence of disease but the complete physical, mental, and social well-being of a person. At Jehangir hospital, this is a belief that is reflected in its predictive, preventive approach to healthcare. It aims at offering solutions that set a very high standard of preventive and curative treatments.
